Hummus

Description

I lived in Israel for 1 year after graduating college. Me and my roommate made it our mission that year to eat as much hummus as possible.

Every weekend we'd get up early, and hop on a bus to whatever city we'd heard might have the best of the best.

We loved arguing with anyone we could about the best spots for hummus

Now, Hummus is my go-to flex. It's easy. It's full of protien. It'll make the goyim feel cultured.

Ingredients

  • Garlic (1 clove)
  • Lemon Juice (1/4 cup)
  • Tahini (16 Ounce)
  • Salt, Cumin
  • Chickpeas canned(30 Ounces)

Steps

  1. In food processor, mix garlic, lemon juice, salt, cumin and tahini
  2. Open chickpeas and drain, save 1/2 cup water
  3. Pour chickpea water into food processor
  4. Process until smooth
  5. Add 2/3 of your chickpeas and blend until smooth
  6. Place in bowl, add remaining chickpeas
